Internal Review & Release Coordination Framework

This Agreement outlines the review framework, procedural coordination, compliance handling, and account transition process relating to the pending withdrawal and release matter currently under evaluation by the involved parties.

Purpose of Agreement

The purpose of this Agreement is to establish a structured path toward resolution of the pending withdrawal matter and to define the responsibilities, review procedures, and remaining coordination requirements associated with the release process.

Account Status

  • The account remains active within the platform system.
  • The matter presently under review relates primarily to withdrawal processing, account transition, banking coordination, and procedural review requirements connected to release handling.
  • Discussions relating to Tier 2 account transition status have previously taken place prior to involvement of Review Counsel.
  • The purpose of this Agreement is to facilitate orderly completion of the remaining procedural and compliance stages necessary for release processing.

Role of Review Counsel

  • Review of account-related records and communications
  • Coordination with involved representatives where necessary
  • Preparation and review of procedural documentation
  • Oversight of release-stage communications and compliance coordination
  • Facilitation of final release-stage review procedures
Notice:

This document reflects the current procedural framework discussed between the involved parties in connection with the account transition and release process.

Tier 2 Transition Framework

The parties acknowledge that progression into Tier 2 account status forms part of the remaining transition and release framework presently under review.

Upon completion of the remaining transition procedures and associated review requirements, the release process may proceed under the updated account structure and coordination framework.

Professional & Administrative Fees

  • Assigned Counsel Legal Fee: USD $4,150.00 (Four Thousand One Hundred Fifty United States Dollars)
  • Remaining Balance Upon Completion: An amount equal to 0.25% (Zero Point Twenty-Five Percent) of the final profit amount released pursuant to the account withdrawal process.

Client Responsibilities

  • Cooperate with reasonable review requests
  • Maintain professional communication throughout the process
  • Review and execute documentation promptly
  • Complete remaining procedural requirements associated with the release framework

Execution and Release Coordination

The parties acknowledge that this Agreement records the understanding reached between the Client, Review Counsel, and Astutegain concerning the remaining requirements associated with the account transition and withdrawal matter.

Upon execution of this Agreement by the Client, Review Counsel, and an authorized representative of Astutegain, and upon completion of the obligations expressly set forth herein, the parties shall proceed with the next stage of the withdrawal release process in accordance with the agreed framework documented by the parties.

The parties further acknowledge that this Agreement is intended to provide clarity, establish the agreed procedures, and facilitate orderly completion of the account transition and withdrawal process.

Astutegain Responsibilities

Astutegain, acting through its authorized representative, shall be responsible for cooperating with the documented release coordination framework and for taking the necessary internal steps required to move the account transition and withdrawal release process forward following full execution of this Agreement.

  • Review and execute this Agreement through an authorized representative.
  • Coordinate with the Client and Review Counsel regarding the account transition and withdrawal release process.
  • Provide timely responses to reasonable requests for information required to complete the procedures described herein.
  • Maintain appropriate internal records of communications, approvals, and actions taken pursuant to this Agreement.
  • Upon completion of the obligations expressly stated in this Agreement, proceed with the agreed release-coordination process in accordance with the documented framework.

Binding Effect and Completion Timeline

Upon execution by all required parties, this Agreement shall constitute a binding procedural understanding between the signing parties with respect to the account transition and release coordination process described herein.

Astutegain, through its authorized representative, shall be responsible for following up on the agreed release coordination steps and shall proceed with the required account-release actions within a period not exceeding thirty (30) calendar days from the date of full execution of this Agreement, provided the Client has completed all obligations expressly stated in this document.
