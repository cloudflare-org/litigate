Withdrawal Release and Account Transition Agreement

Prepared under legal review and regulatory coordination framework

This Withdrawal Release and Account Transition Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into between the undersigned account holder (“Client”), the relevant representatives associated with Astutegain (“Platform Representatives”), and the reviewing legal/compliance representative coordinating the matter (“Review Counsel”).

This Agreement is prepared in connection with compliance review considerations, procedural communications, and regulatory coordination related to the Client’s pending withdrawal matter and account transition process.

Purpose of Agreement

This Agreement is intended to formally outline the remaining procedural requirements, responsibilities, compliance understandings, and release framework connected to the Client’s pending withdrawal matter and account transition process.

The parties acknowledge that concerns were previously raised regarding delays and procedural handling connected to the withdrawal process. Following discussions involving Platform Representatives, Review Counsel, and regulatory compliance-related communications, this Agreement is intended to establish a structured path toward final resolution and release.

Regulatory Review and Coordination

The parties acknowledge that aspects of the withdrawal matter and account transition process have involved compliance-related communications and procedural review considerations associated with the United States Securities and Exchange Commission (“SEC”).

Review Counsel has coordinated procedural and compliance-related communications connected to the present release framework. This Agreement reflects the present procedural understanding reached following legal review, compliance coordination, and SEC-related regulatory communications connected to the matter.
